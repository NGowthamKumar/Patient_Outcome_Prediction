Breat Cancer Prediction

Breast cancer is a prevalent form of cancer that affects both men and women, with women being more commonly diagnosed. It develops when cells in the breast begin to grow uncontrollably, forming a mass or lump. While breast cancer can occur at any age, it is most frequently diagnosed in women over the age of 50.
